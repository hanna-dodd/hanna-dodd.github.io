I'm Hanna Dodd, an MMath student in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. My research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, digital embodiment, and narrative identity in tabletop role-playing games (TTRPGs).

I enjoy building interactive tools and experiments that push the boundaries of how games and storytelling can be experienced. My academic work is influenced by a deep love of fantasy, systems thinking, and creative technology.

Outside of research, I enjoy running emotionally rich D&D campaigns, rock climbing, and exploring how technology reshapes our sense of self and story.
